# Joe Lanman

Hi, I’m an interaction designer based in London.

I have over 15 years experience designing digital services based on user needs
and behaviours, and I’ve been at the UK Government Digital Service since 2013.

My passion is creating simple, accessible and beautiful services that improve
people’s lives.
